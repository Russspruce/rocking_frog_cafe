Our Story


Established in 2007, The Rocking Frog Cafe is independently run and nestled in the heart of Belmont Street in South East Portland.


Serving Ristretto Roasters, we believe in preparing an excellent cup of coffee that is refined for the connoisseur, yet accessible to the casual drinker. With games, an eclectic library for children and adults, a full menu, excellent pastries, and free Internet access, you will never be at a loss for something to enjoy. Grab a cup on the way to work or put your feet up by the fire and enjoy a lazy day.
